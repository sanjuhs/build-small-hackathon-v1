Demo story

Fire Boy is a tiny model-backed toy with a real build trail.

Toy Room v3 routes typed or spoken commands through a bounded PET action contract, then turns model output into speech, animation, physics, camera traces, and room objects.

Modal is used for serverless MiniCPM-o inference, while the VLA path keeps MiniCPM-V 4.6, policy heads, and RunPod rollout artifacts visible as training proof instead of hidden notes.
